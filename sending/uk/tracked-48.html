Royal Mail Tracked 48®

Peace of mind with end-to-end tracking

Features

  • End-to-end tracking
  • SMS or email notifications to recipients
  • Aims to deliver in two to three working days
  • Compensation cover up to £100
     

Options

  • Signature on delivery
  • Flexible delivery options available - including Safeplace & Delivery to Neighbour

Please note

Due to the current coronavirus situation, we're no longer asking the recipient to sign for items delivered on or after Saturday 14 March 2020.

The recipient's name and confirmation of delivery can still be viewed at www.royalmail.com/track-your-item

Find out more at www.royalmail.com/coronavirus

How to use this service

Buy online with Click & Drop

Tracked 24 and 48 can only be bought online. You can stick your label to your item, or we can print if for you for free at a Customer Service Point where you can drop your item off too. Help with where you can post/drop off your item

 

Or why not buy postage using our new app? Get the App

Prices, terms and conditions

How to buy and post
  1. Buy Tracked 24 and 48 online using Click & Drop
  2. Print your postage at home, or bring your item to a Customer Service Point and we'll print it for you, scanning the QR code you’ve received in your email confirmation
  3. Drop off your item at the Customer Service Point too. Please note - you can't post items using Tracked 24 and 48 at a Post Office

Prohibited and restricted items

We restrict or prohibit certain items from our network. You are advised to carefully read the restrictions on items like:

  • Batteries
  • Aerosols
  • Perfumes or aftershaves
  • Nail varnish, polish or gel
