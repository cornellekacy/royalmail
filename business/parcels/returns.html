Parcel returns services

Reliable returns services help you stay up-to-date with stock – while keeping customers happy, so they come back time and time again

Easy returns for happy customers

2 in 3 shoppers expect their returns to be free*, and they want the process to be easy and convenient too.

With our reliable returns, stock is back with you quickly for re-sale, plus you can process any refunds promptly – keeping buyers happy, and making them more likely to shop with you again.

65% of online shoppers prefer to return items via the Post Office®*. You can provide a barcoded return label in their parcel – or let them print their own.

With Labels to Go your shoppers don’t even need a printer – they can drop in to a Post Office or Customer Service Point and print the returns label there.

Returns services for business

End-to-end tracking

Peace of mind for you and your customers

  • Manage stock easily by keeping track of returned items as they make their way back to your warehouse
  • Issue refunds or replacements faster
  • Sign up to our online returns portal so your customers can download labels easily. They can print labels free of charge at a Post Office branch or Customer Service Point, and drop their items off at the same time

Guaranteed next-day returns by 1pm

The fastest returns service

  • Online tracking, plus signature on delivery for extra security
  • Send pre-printed return envelopes for convenience
  • Customers can choose whether to drop parcels off, or send them with a scheduled business collection

Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed Returns®

Cost-effective overseas returns

Encourage overseas shoppers to buy from the UK with confidence

  • Choose from part-paid or fully-paid delivery options
  • Available from 28 countries, from Europe to New Zealand
  • We aim to deliver items back to you in 7–10 days
