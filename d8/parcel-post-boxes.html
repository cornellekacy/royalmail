Easy, convenient parcel drop off

If you’re returning an item or buying postage online with Click & Drop or the Royal Mail App, you can now drop them off at a Parcel Postbox near you.

Parcel Postboxes are ideally suited for a fast and easy local drop off, and when a Certificate of Posting is not required.

  • If you’re returning an item, simply stick your return label on your item and drop it off
  • If you’re sending an item, go to Click & Drop, or the Royal Mail App

How it works

  1. Buy postage online
  2. Print your label at home
  3. Drop off your item at your local Parcel Postbox

Check this page regularly to keep up to date with the latest Parcel Postboxes that are ready to use in your area.

Please note that the maximum Parcel size allowed into our new Postboxes is 44cm x 35cm x 16cm. Any Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed items, Royal Mail International Tracked items, and any Royal Mail Tracked Returns without a barcode on the label must be posted in a Post Office branch and cannot be dropped off at a Parcel Postbox.
